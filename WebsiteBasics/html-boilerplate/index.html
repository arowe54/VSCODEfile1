Notes:

index.html is the homepage. !DOCTYPE html gets HTML5 (latest version).

html is the root element. lang specifies language (en = english). meta charset="utf-8" makes sure website displays special symbols correctly.

Head gives important meta-information about the webpage plus stuff to render correctly in the browser. Don't put elements that display content in head.

Body holds all content displayed to users. Use p for paragraphs and to add white space and breaks on website. White space on code does not mean white space on webpage. Press ! and first option for a boiler plate shortcut.

You could use an html validator online to check your code for syntax errors.

There are 6 heading styles from h1 to h6 (large to small)

strong makes text bold. em makes text italic and places emphasis on text. strong and em can make screen readers change their voice
